Leavitt Township Citizens

This is NOT THE OFFICIAL WEBSITE for Leavitt Township, Oceana County, Michigan.

The township board when asked repeatedly by myself would not publish meeting minutes, ordinances and other documents on their public website, http://leavitttownship.org.

The stated reason per Emma Kirwin, the township supervisor, for not posting the laws and meeting notes they are creating for this community is "liability advice from their lawyer", yet documents were being posted on the website previously and were removed. Access is currently provided by emailing Naomi Oomen, the township clerk.

Instead of arguing that this is purposefully obstructionist I am publishing all minutes and related documents on this website as I get them in my spare time, to the best of my ability.
